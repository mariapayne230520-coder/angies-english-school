Our Mission

At Angie's English School we are dedicated to teaching English to students of all ages through a specialized methodology for Spanish speakers, which connects learning with the culture and structure of the Spanish language. Our goal is to develop communication, understanding and confidence skills in each student, using dynamic, practical and personalized strategies that facilitate the natural transition from Spanish to English. We train students capable of developing fluently and confidently in academic, professional and cultural environments at a global level.

Our Vision

To be the leading English school in the comprehensive training of Spanish speakers, recognized for its innovation, academic quality and verifiable results, preparing students of all ages to communicate fluently and open up to global opportunities, without losing their cultural identity.

Our Values

  • Academic Excellence: We guarantee a high level of teaching with updated and effective methods.
  • Adaptability: We adjust our strategies to the needs, ages and learning rhythms of each student.
  • Cultural Proximity: We teach English respecting and taking advantage of the linguistic and cultural roots of Spanish.
  • Innovation: We incorporate modern resources and technologies for dynamic and attractive learning.
  • Commitment: We accompany our students until they reach their linguistic goals.
  • Trust: We promote a safe environment to learn, practice and make mistakes without fear.
  • Teamwork: We collaborate with families, teachers and students to achieve lasting results.
